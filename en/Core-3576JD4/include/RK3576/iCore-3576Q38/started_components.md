# Introduction
**Core-3576JD4** Equipped with Rockchip octa-core AIoT processor RK3576, supports private deployment of modern mainstream large models and decoding of up to 8K video. With a compact size of just 38 × 38mm, this SoM integrates 6 TOPS NPU and features industrial-grade innovations such as real-time networking, Flexbus, hardware resource isolation, and DSMC.
