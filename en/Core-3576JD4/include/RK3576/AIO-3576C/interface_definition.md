# Hardware Interfaces

## Board Interface Specification

**AIO-3576JD4 V1.0** interfaces include:

* 1 x HDMI2.1 (Up to 4K120Hz)
* 1 x USB3.0 OTG (Type-C)
* 1 x USB3.0
* 2 x USB2.0 (One Of Them On 4P-1.25mm)
* 1 x MIPI-DSI
* 1 x MIPI-D/CPHY-CSI
* 2 x MIPI-DPHY-CSI
* 1 x TF Card
* 1 x M.2 (SATA3.1 / NVME)
* 1 x WIFI
* 1 x Bluetooth
* 1 x FAN
* 1 x RJ45 (up to 1Gbps)
* 1 × 3.5mm Headphone Jack
* 1 × Mic Input (2P-1.25mm)
* 1 × SPEAKER (4P-1.25mm)
* 1 x DEBUG TTL
* 1 x RS485
* 1 x RS232
* 1 x CAN
* 1 x Mini PCIe (4G Module)
* 1 x eDP 40Pin/30Pin (Multiplexing with HDMI, not supported by default)

## Special Notice
MIPI-DSI and LVDS cannot be used at the same time. 

eDP and HDMI multiplexing, default HDMI, if you need eDP support, you can contact the official consultation.
