# Hardware Interfaces

## Board Interface Specification

**EXT-iCore-3576Q38 V0.2** interfaces include:

* 1 x Type-C (Device Mode)
* 1 x USB2.0 (One Of 24Pin On Extension interface)
* 1 x MIPI-DPHY-CSI
* 1 x TF Card
* 1 x WIFI
* 1 x Bluetooth
* 1 x Ethernet（up to 100Mbps）
* 1 × Mic Input（2P-1.25mm）
* 1 × Speaker Out（4P-1.25mm）
* 1 x RS485
* 1 x TTL (One Of 24Pin On Extension interface)
* 1 x RTC battery (4P-1.25mm)
