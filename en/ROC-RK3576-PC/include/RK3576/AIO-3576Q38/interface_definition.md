# Hardware Interfaces

## Board Interface Specification

**ROC-RK3576-PC V1.0** interfaces include:

* 1 x HDMI2.1（Up to 4K120Hz）
* 1 x USB3.0 OTG(Type-C)
* 2 x USB3.0
* 1 x MIPI-D/CPHY-CSI
* 1 x MIPI-DPHY-CSI
* 1 x TF Card
* 1 x M.2(SATA3.1)
* 1 x WIFI
* 1 x Bluetooth
* 1 x FAN
* 2 x RJ45（up to 1Gbps）
* 1 x RS485
* 1 x RS232
* 1 x CAN
* 1 x Mini PCIe (4G Module)
* 1 x PCIe M.2 (5G Module)

## Special Notice
Mini PCIe (4G Module) and PCIe M.2 (5G Module) used same USB bus, so they can not be used at same time.

Unavailable function:
* 1 x MIPI-DSI
* 1 x MIPI-DPHY-CSI (CSI3/4)
* 1 × 3.5mm Headphone Jack
* 1 × Mic Input（2P-1.25mm）
* 1 x Bluetooth (SAI2)
* Extension Pins
