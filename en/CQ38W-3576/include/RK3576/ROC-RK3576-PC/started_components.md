# Introduction
ROC-RK3576-PC is based on the Rockchip second-generation 8nm high-performance AIoT chip -RK3576, equipped with eight-core (Cortex-A72 x 4 + Cortex-A53 x 4) 64-bit CPU, the main frequency of up to 2.2GHz, It provides strong support for high performance computing and multi-task processing.

Built-in ARM Mali G52 MC3 GPU, integrated 6 TOPS computing power NPU, support the privatization of large-scale parameter model deployment under Transformer architecture, support a variety of deep learning frameworks, custom operator development, Docker containerization management technology. The external watchdog provides industrial stability and is widely applicable to local AI deployment scenarios.
